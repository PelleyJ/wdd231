Gear Up Right

Protect yourself with quality gear on every ride.

Recommended Essentials

  • Helmet: Always wear a DOT-approved full-face helmet for maximum protection.
  • Riding Jacket: Choose armored jackets with abrasion resistance and ventilation.
  • Gloves: Go for padded gloves with knuckle protection and grip control.
  • Boots: Ankle-high boots with non-slip soles improve safety.
  • Pants: Kevlar-reinforced or armored pants shield against impact and road rash.
  • Rain Gear: Keep a compact waterproof set for unexpected weather changes.
